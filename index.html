How to Play

  • Pick a category and a point value
  • Click on the chosen box for the question
  • You will be given 15 seconds to answer the question
  • You must choose an answer or wait till time is up before choosing another question
  • If question is answered correctly, you will be awarded the points for that question to your total score
  • If answered incorrectly or if time runs out, you will lose that questions point worth from your total score
